# User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Enterprise Support Assistant as an employee to get answers about company policies, benefits, and procedures.

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. **Access the application** at your organization's designated URL
2. **Enter your credentials**:
   - Username: Your employee username or email
   - Password: Your assigned password
3. **Click Login** to access the chat interface

### First Time Login

When you first log in, you'll see:
- Your employee information automatically populated (name, department, employee ID)
- A welcome message explaining how to use the system
- A list of available company documents on the left sidebar
- An empty chat area ready for your questions

## Using the Chat Interface

### Asking Questions

The AI assistant can help you with various workplace topics:

**HR and Benefits Questions:**
- "What is our PTO policy?"
- "How do I request sick leave?"
- "What health insurance options do we have?"
- "When is the next enrollment period?"

**IT and Security Policies:**
- "What's the password policy?"
- "How do I report a security incident?"
- "Can I use personal devices for work?"
- "What software am I allowed to install?"

**General Company Policies:**
- "What's the dress code?"
- "How do I submit an expense report?"
- "What's the policy on remote work?"
- "Who do I contact for facility issues?"

### Understanding Responses

Each response includes:

1. **Direct Answer**: A clear, conversational response to your question
2. **Source References**: Links to the specific company documents that contain the information
3. **Document Highlights**: Click on document links to see the exact sections with your answer highlighted

### Tips for Better Results

**Be Specific:**
- ✅ "How many vacation days do I get in my second year?"
- ❌ "Tell me about time off"

**Ask Follow-up Questions:**
- The system remembers your conversation context
- You can ask for clarification or additional details
- Example: "What about sick leave?" after asking about vacation

**Use Natural Language:**
- Ask questions as you would to a colleague
- The AI understands context and can interpret various phrasings

## Navigation Features

### Document Viewer

- **Browse Documents**: See all available company documents in the left sidebar
- **Document Details**: View file type, size, and last modified date
- **Search Highlighting**: When you click a document reference, it opens with your search terms highlighted
- **New Tab Opening**: Documents open in new tabs so you don't lose your chat

### User Profile

Your profile information is automatically displayed:
- **Employee ID**: Your unique identifier
- **Full Name**: As recorded in the system
- **Department**: Your organizational department
- **Position**: Your job title

### Chat Management

- **Reset Chat**: Clear your conversation history and start fresh
- **Conversation Memory**: The system remembers context within your session
- **Logout**: Securely end your session when finished

## Troubleshooting

### If You Can't Log In

1. **Check your credentials** - Ensure username and password are correct
2. **Clear browser cookies** - Sometimes old session data interferes
3. **Try a different browser** - Test with Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
4. **Contact IT support** - If issues persist, reach out to your IT department

### If Responses Seem Inaccurate

1. **Check the source documents** - Click on referenced documents to verify information
2. **Ask for clarification** - Request more specific information or different phrasing
3. **Escalate if needed** - For critical policy questions, confirm with HR or management

### If Documents Won't Open

1. **Check your popup blocker** - Allow popups for the application site
2. **Try right-click and "Open in New Tab"** - Manual navigation if auto-open fails
3. **Download the document** - Some browsers may require manual download

## Best Practices

### Effective Question Asking

1. **Start with specific topics**: "vacation policy" rather than "time off"
2. **Include relevant details**: Your department, employment status, or specific situation
3. **Ask one question at a time**: This helps get more focused responses
4. **Follow up for clarity**: Don't hesitate to ask for more details

### Privacy and Security

1. **Don't share personal information** unnecessarily in questions
2. **Log out when finished** especially on shared computers
3. **Report suspicious activity** if you notice unusual system behavior
4. **Keep your password secure** and don't share credentials

### Getting the Most Value

1. **Explore different question styles** to find what works best
2. **Use the document viewer** to understand the source material
3. **Save important information** from your chats for future reference
4. **Provide feedback** to administrators about system usefulness

## Limitations

### What the System Can Do

- Answer questions about existing company policies and procedures
- Provide summaries and explanations of company documents
- Help you find specific information quickly
- Maintain conversation context for follow-up questions

### What the System Cannot Do

- Make policy decisions or exceptions
- Access your personal employment records
- Approve requests or applications
- Provide legal advice beyond company policy
- Update or modify company policies

### When to Escalate

Contact your supervisor, HR, or IT directly when you need:
- **Personal account access** - Password resets, system access issues
- **Policy exceptions** - Situations not covered by standard policies
- **Urgent matters** - Time-sensitive issues requiring immediate attention
- **Confidential discussions** - Sensitive personal or professional matters

## Support

If you need help using the Enterprise Support Assistant:

1. **Try the built-in help** - Ask the AI "How do I use this system?"
2. **Check with colleagues** - Other users may have helpful tips
3. **Contact your IT department** - For technical issues or access problems
4. **Reach out to HR** - For questions about policy coverage or accuracy

Remember: The Enterprise Support Assistant is designed to help you find information quickly and efficiently, but it's always okay to follow up with human experts when you need additional clarification or support.
